## Project Overview

Spot Edit is an AI-powered document editor that enables intelligent, context-aware editing through natural language commands. The project focuses on seamlessly integrating AI assistance into the writing workflow, with "spot-targeting" as a core feature - allowing users to select specific areas of documents for focused AI intervention.
